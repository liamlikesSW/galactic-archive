In addition to tracking all the worlds in Star Wars canon, I’ve also begun tracking the newly created solar systems and planetary sectors since the 2014 canon reboot. Solar systems are what they sound like; all the worlds that orbit one star (or multiple in some cases) are in that one system. Sectors contain many

systems and are established by artificial, conventional boundaries and usually have a planetary capital. The galaxy is divided into a little over a thousand sectors. I did not bother to sort this list into anything other than alphabetical, but you can still see an entry’s regional placement below their name if they have one.
