The Mid Rim of the galaxy is the region between the Expansion Region and the Outer Rim Territories. Although the Mid Rim encompasses many remote systems, there are some worlds whose cultures’ and histories are just as influential as many Core Worlds, such as Naboo in the Chommell sector. During the Clone Wars, the Mid Rim saw extensive fighting with the battles on Bracca,

Malastare, Kashyyyk, and many more worlds. During the Galactic Civil War, the Mid Rim was sympathetic to the Rebel Alliance. Because of this, the Alliance made an attempt to take the region, calling the campaign the Mid Rim Offensive. The operation ultimately stalled, but the Alliance made many new allies that later helped them defeat the Empire after the Battle of Endor.

Nakadia

j   M I D   R I M   j

An agricultural world, it had a unique soil composition that allows it to grow a variety of vital crops. Nakadia was home to many farms. It's cities and town were small compared to other locations in the galaxy. During the Clone Wars, Nakadia was occupied by the Separatists, who brought waves of battle droids. As a result, the Nakadians developed a bias against droids. Due to its pristine environment, Nakadia was classified by the New Republic as a

Class A protected planet. Restricted agricultural products like pta fruits were not allowed on Nakadia since they were considered invasive. The planet's capital was Quarrow, which had a few thousand inhabitants. Quarrow's fibercrete streets were designed for biological only and were unsuitable for droids, speeders, and machines. It was voted capital of the New Republic for one year, initiated not long before the Battle of Jakku.

(Battlefront: Twilight Company)

Shantipole

 M I D   R I M

A remote world, Shantipole had a violent atmosphere of gases and lightning that it was practically desolate. The planet's surface was dominated by mountain spires and large winged creatures known as dactillions could be found in the skies. Shantipole was once a highly prospected source of rare gases similar to tibanna, however investors abandoned Shantipole due to its unpredictable wind patterns. Because of that violent atmosphere, most pilots typically avoided Shantipole, often regarded it as a "one-way trip."

This attribute, however, made it the perfect workplace for Quarrie, a Mon Calamari master engineer and starfighter designer, as it allowed him to toil on his creations far from the watchful eye of the Galactic Empire. He established a secret starfighter laboratory and, after years of work, he designed and built Prototype B6, an advanced starfighter armed with experimental weapons that would later become a mainstay of the Rebel Alliance.

(Star Wars: Rebels - 'Wings of the Master')
