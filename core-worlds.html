The wealthiest, most prestigious worlds in the galaxy.  Those who inhabited the Core Worlds usually had a distinctly clipped, superior accent, and were often regarded as snobs.  The capital of the galaxy for centuries under the leadership of the Galactic Republic, and later, the Galactic Empire was located on Coruscant, the most well known of the ecumenopoli in the Core. When the Galactic Republic was formed about one thousand years before

the onslaught of the Clone Wars,  the Core Worlds became ravenous. Granted permission to settle new planets and establish trading colonies, these worlds either cut deals with indigenous populations or simply overran them. With the rise of Mon Mothma's New Republic, the capital planet was elected each year by the Senate, occasionally taking the center of government far from the Core.
