Located between the Core Worlds and the Inner Rim, the Colonies Region of the galaxy sat along major trade routes, and due to this, some of them were extremely wealthy. During the Clone Wars several battles reached this region of the galaxy.  Towards the end of the war on Cato Neimoidia, the

Trade Federation's capital world, the Republic fought a prolonged campaign against Separatist forces led by Nute Gunray. On Devaron, Count Dooku sent droid forces to cease the Jedi Temple of Eedit and met considerable resistance.
